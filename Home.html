Why the HBO-ICT program at HZ is the right choice for me?

Upon contemplating as to why the ICT program at HZ is the most suitable course for me, it is the practical approach of learning about computers and technology that made me inclined to join this course. I love learning and solving problems, and I believe having this drive to solve problems by the means of technology is what is needed and expected to succeed in this course. Although I don't have programming experience I did explore the field by taking a few courses on Java and python from Udemy and Coursera. To have the desire to try new things and to challenge oneself to be better in what they do are the qualities that are needed to succeed in this course and I believe I am sufficiently equipped with the required qualities.

My fundamental interest is to study core CS programming as it will help me to

  • design and develop software systems, web services and mobile applications.
  • I would like to use these skills to help build new technology and products that can help society create a more environmental friendly lifestyle.
