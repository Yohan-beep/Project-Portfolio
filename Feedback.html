SWOT:

What strengths from being a good sportsman can be implemented in everyday study life? What can you use from having to train / care for dogs in your everyday study life? how is being an introvert a weakness? what would it make a strength? How can you use others or a system to pull you out of the details and back to the greater picture? How can you use others to maintain your motivation ?

1. Being active in sports helps in developing a positive mindset. It helps in keeping the mind fresh and also plays a role in moulding and developing good qualities that every human being should have.

2. Taking care of my dogs helps in keeping the mind fresh and overall helps in having a good mood to work or study.

3. Being an introvert is not always a sign of weakness but not building relationships or trying new things with different people in a new place is not good either. However, it helps in having focus on the work you do as you dont pay much heed to what others are doing.

4. I stop thinking for a moment after which I take a blank piece of paper and just write down the main points of what I am working on. This helps get more clarity as to what I am working on and what needs to be done to achieve my goals in a much more efficient manner.

5. Seeing how well others do well and excel either academically or in sports is a great source of motivationas I too wish to be the best in what I do.
